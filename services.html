Physiotherapy Services

At Ascent Physiotherapy we offer a wide range of Physiotherapy Services aimed at ensuring you live a thriving, healthy life.

Manual and Manipulative Therapy

Manual Therapy techniques (joint and muscle stretching and mobilizing) are often used for relief of pain, swelling and other uncomfortable symptoms.

Book Now

Intramuscular Stimulation (IMS)

Targeted needling to release muscle tension and alleviate pain in specific areas of the body.

Book Now

Acupuncture

Medical Acupuncture is often incorporated into the physiotherapy treatment. Acupuncture can provide instantaneous pain relief, accelerating a return to normal movement.

Book Now

Radial Shockwave Therapy

Non-invasive treatment using shockwaves to stimulate healing and reduce pain for various musculoskeletal conditions.

Book Now

Treatment for TMJ Dysfunction

Specialized therapy to address temporomandibular joint issues, relieving pain and improving jaw function.

Book Now

Myofascial Release and Craniosacral Therapy

Techniques to release tension in the connective tissues (myofascia) and promote balance in the craniosacral system.

Book Now

Sports Therapy

If you recently suffered a sports-related injury and are looking for a customize physiotherapy plan, please call in and schedule an appointment so we can work together on a custom solution to fit your needs.

Book Now

Athletic Taping/Bracing

Application of specialized taping or bracing techniques to support joints and muscles, aiding in injury prevention and recovery.
