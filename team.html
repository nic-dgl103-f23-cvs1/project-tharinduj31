Meet The Team

Meet our dedicated physiotherapy team – experienced professionals committed to enhancing your well-being through personalized care and comprehensive rehabilitation services.

Physio Therapists

Kendra Mulligan

With 28 years of physiotherapy expertise, Kendra, a University of Alberta alumna, excels in musculoskeletal care. Co-owner of Ascent Physiotherapy in Comox, BC, she holds FCAMPT certification and actively engages in community sports. Passionate about mountain biking, she's certified in acupuncture and GunnIMS. Kendra's commitment to clinical excellence extends to her role as a teaching assistant for the Orthopedic division of the Canadian Physiotherapy Association. Her dedication, combined with evidence-based practices, has significantly contributed to the well-being of the Comox Valley community.

Trish Leslie

Trish, a 2011 graduate with honors from Queen Margaret University in Edinburgh, holds a Bachelor’s of Science in Physical Therapy. Her diverse physiotherapy career spans orthopaedics, women's health, geriatrics, neurology, and sports physiotherapy. Trish has contributed her expertise to rugby clubs in Edinburgh and Victoria. With a focus on manual therapies, modalities, and exercise prescription, she has worked in private practice and acute care since 2012. Certified in acupuncture, Trish's commitment to professional development reflects her dedication to providing comprehensive care. Originally from Ucluelet, she's delighted to call the Comox Valley home.

Jared Hromika

Jared, a 2014 University of British Columbia Master's of Physical Therapy graduate, joined the Comox Valley in 2015. His diverse background includes locum positions in Vancouver's private practice orthopaedics. Jared's treatment approach integrates movement assessment, manual therapy, and exercise prescription. Trained in Neurokinetic Therapy, he addresses compensation patterns for enhanced treatment outcomes. Jared, passionate about triathlons since 2006, extends his expertise to bike fitting at Ascent Physiotherapy. Beyond the clinic, he engages in swimming, trail running, softball, hockey, and enjoys optimizing bike efficiency and comfort.

Shadi Fleifel

Shadi, a valued member of the Ascent Physiotherapy Team since 2018, holds a Bachelor's in Kinesiology from Western University and a Master's in Physiotherapy from Perth, Australia (2008). With over a decade of experience, he's worked with premier rugby clubs and the Canada Rugby 7's team. Specializing in sports rehabilitation, Shadi excels in treating diverse injuries. Committed to professional growth, he has pursued various courses, enhancing his skills. Outside the clinic, Shadi enjoys family time, engaging in crossfit, swimming, biking, running, rugby, basketball, and surfing.

Chelsey de la Rey

Chelsey, a Comox Valley native, earned her Bachelor of Human Kinetics from Trinity Western University and her Master's of Physical Therapy from the University of British Columbia in 2019. Dedicated to individualized care, she emphasizes comprehensive assessments, manual therapy, exercise prescription, and education for self-management. Chelsey, involved in sports from varsity competition to coaching, has completed Orthopedic Division Level 1 and aspires to further education in manual therapy and pain science. Outside the clinic, she enjoys golf, fishing, gym workouts, and actively participates in sports like curling, basketball, and volleyball with her husband.

Carter Mulligan

Carter, a Registered BCRPA Fitness Leader, holds certifications in Personal Training, Weight Training, and Fitness Theory. Recognized by BCRPA, he adheres to the national standard for fitness leadership. With a passion for bodybuilding and three years of intensive training, Carter brings a competitive background in lacrosse and rugby.
